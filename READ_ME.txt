Breakbot for Microsoft Teams
Last updated: 01/10/2024

Breakbot is a bot for Microsoft Temas that helps manage and track break times.
An egg timer if you will.


Features:
Start, extend and stop breaks.
Notifications about ongoing breaks.

Data Handling:
